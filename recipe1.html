Taco Pasta

This is a delicious recipe for Taco Pasta. It's a fusion of Mexican and Italian

Ingredients:

  • 1 lb. ground beef
  • 1/2 small onion, chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 packet taco seasoning
  • 1/2 cup water
  • 1/2 cup salsa
  • 1/2 cup sour cream
  • 1/2 cup shredded cheddar cheese
  • 8 oz. pasta
  • 1/2 cup shredded cheddar cheese
  • 1/2 cup shredded Monterey Jack cheese
  • 1/4 cup chopped tomatoes
  • 1/4 cup chopped green onions

Instructions:

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F.
  2. In a large skillet, cook ground beef over medium heat until no longer pink. Drain fat.
  3. Add onion and garlic and cook until onion is soft.
  4. Stir in taco seasoning, water, salsa, sour cream, and 1/2 cup cheddar cheese.
  5. Simmer for 5 minutes.
  6. Meanwhile, cook pasta according to package directions.
  7. Drain pasta and add to the meat mixture.
  8. Stir to combine.
  9. Transfer to a 9x13-inch baking dish.
  10. Sprinkle remaining cheddar and Monterey Jack cheese on top.
  11. Bake for 15-20 minutes or until cheese is melted and bubbly.
  12. Top with tomatoes and green onions.
