Chicken Alfredo

This is a delicious recipe for Chicken Alfredo. It's a classic Italian dish that's creamy and satisfying.

Ingredients:

  • 1 lb. fettuccine
  • 2 boneless, skinless chicken breasts
  • 1/2 tsp. garlic powder
  • 1/2 tsp. onion powder
  • 1/2 tsp. paprika
  • 1/2 tsp. salt
  • 1/4 tsp. pepper
  • 2 Tbsp. olive oil
  • 1/2 cup butter
  • 1 cup heavy cream
  • 1 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1/4 cup chopped parsley

Instructions:

  1. Cook fettuccine according to package directions.
  2. Season chicken with garlic powder, onion powder, paprika, salt, and pepper.
  3. Heat olive oil in a large skillet over medium heat.
  4. Add chicken and cook until no longer pink.
  5. Remove chicken from skillet and set aside.
  6. In the same skillet, melt butter.
  7. Add heavy cream and bring to a simmer.
  8. Stir in Parmesan cheese until melted.
  9. Return chicken to skillet and simmer until heated through.
  10. Stir in parsley.
  11. Serve over fettuccine.
