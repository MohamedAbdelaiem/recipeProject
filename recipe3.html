Pizza margrita

This is a delicious recipe for Pizza margrita. It's a classic Italian pizza that's simple and flavorful.

Ingredients:

  • 1 lb. pizza dough
  • 1/2 cup marinara sauce
  • 8 oz. fresh mozzarella, sliced
  • 1/4 cup fresh basil leaves
  • 1/4 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1/4 cup olive oil
  • 1/4 tsp. salt
  • 1/4 tsp. pepper

Instructions:

  1. Preheat oven to 450 degrees F.
  2. Roll out pizza dough on a floured surface.
  3. Transfer to a baking sheet.
  4. Spread marinara sauce over dough.
  5. Top with mozzarella slices.
  6. Bake for 15-20 minutes or until crust is golden and cheese is bubbly.
  7. Top with basil leaves and Parmesan cheese.
  8. Drizzle with olive oil and season with salt and pepper.
